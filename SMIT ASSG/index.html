HTMLis like a blueprint for your website. It builds the basic structure by telling the computer what goes where: headings, paragraphs, pictures, links, and more. It doesn't make it fancy, just lays the foundation. Think of it like the skeleton of your website, waiting for the skin and muscles (CSS and JavaScript) to bring it to life!


CSS is like the makeup artist for your website! It takes the plain HTML structure and adds color, style, and personality. It allows you to control things like font size, colors, backgrounds, and layout, making your website visually appealing and user-friendly. Think of it as the finishing touch that transforms a barebones website into a beautiful and engaging experience.


JavaScript is the electrician: It brings the house to life by wiring up lights, appliances, and interactive features. It makes things move, respond, and change based on user actions.
