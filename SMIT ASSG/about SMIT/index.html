Our Mission

To equip youth with essential IT skills, empowering them to become productive members of the digital economy and contribute to Pakistan's technological advancement.

Key Features

  • Free of cost training for eligible candidates
  • Hands-on experience in various IT domains
  • Qualified and experienced instructors
  • Industry-aligned curriculum
  • Job placement assistance

Course Offerings

We offer a wide range of courses, including:

  • Web Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • Network Administration
  • Graphic Design
  • Digital Marketing
  • Cyber Security

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the program, candidates must:

  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Have completed a minimum of matriculation (10th grade)
  • Demonstrate a strong interest in IT

Application Process

Interested candidates can apply online or visit their nearest Saylani Mass IT Training Center.
